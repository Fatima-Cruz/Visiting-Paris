Welcome to Paris


Paris is the capital of France, located in the north-central part of the country. When you hear about Paris, one may begin to think of the Louvre, Notre Dame, and of course the Eiffel Tower. Naturally, it has been prestigious in terms of fashion, pastries, and being known as "The City of Love". Cost of Living Survey in 2018, Paris was the second most expensive city in the world, after Singapore. Similarly, Paris was ranked as the second most visited travel destination in the world in 2019, after Bangkok. After counting the numbers in hotel stays in Paris, there were about 38 million visitors in 2019. The culture of Paris prides itself in its monuments, art, theatre, literature, music, muesumes, and much more. Famous attractions include The Eiffel Tower, the Notre Dame cathedral, the Louvre art museum, Arc de Triomphe monument, and the underground Catacombs of Paris.
